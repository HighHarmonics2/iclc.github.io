Maquinaturalis

Soon we are having access to the 2021 version of Maquinaturalis exhibition as part of the ICLC 2021 programme.

Maquinaturalis is an online exhibition of Latin-American artists questioning human and machine learning relationships by exploring the impact of virtual spaces on the interactions of users and web browsers. Organises and curated by ToplapMX.

To get an idea, visit Maquinaturalis 2020 version here: https://www.maquinaturalis.art/
