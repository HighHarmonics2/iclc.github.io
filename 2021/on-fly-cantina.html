On-the-fly cantina

On-the-fly cantina is a series of casual meetings in the form of video chats. Organised by the on-the-fly research group, the meetings are carried monthly and hosted by one or more members of the community.

In the context of ICLC 2021, we are having special sessions to be announced soon in this web site. Stay tuned to the forthcoming schedule of this particular events aiming to discuss aesthetics, meta levels and how persons relate to live coding both as artists and as audience.

While we prepare the programme, you can get an idea of on-the-fly cantina meetings here: https://onthefly.space/read/on-the-fly-cantina

International Conference on Live Coding
