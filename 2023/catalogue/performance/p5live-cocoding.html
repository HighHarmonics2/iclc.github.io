Program Notes

At this participatory performance the barrier between the audience and the performers will be blurred. Rather than facing the audience, the performers will join them as multiple machines invite anyone to join the P5LIVE COCODING session. Starting from scratch, the shared codebase will extend, break, tweak, grow in complexity, and ideally bring us all to new unexpected visual territories. So join in and COCODE with us!
