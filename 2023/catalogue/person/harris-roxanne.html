Biography

Roxanne Harris, alsoknownasrox, is a multidisciplinary artist and educator based in Brooklyn, New York. She approaches programming as a medium for creative expression. Roxanne specializes in programming as performance, modifying real-time processes to create dynamic audiovisual experiences. She leads workshops and organizes shows as an active member of LiveCode.NYC.
