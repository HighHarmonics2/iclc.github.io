Address and phone number of ICLC 2017 venues

ICLC 2017 will be held in the historic city of Morelia, Michoacán, located in the central region of Mexico. The Conference will take place in 3 important cultural centers: the Centro Mexicano para la Música y las Artes Sonoras (CMMAS), the Centro Cultural Clavijero, and the Comité de Asuntos Intangibles.

Traveling to Morelia from outside Mexico

To travel to Morelia from a differnt country than Mexico you need to flight to Benito Juarez international airport (MEX) located in Mexico City and then take either a flight to Morelia's General Francisco Mújica international airport (MLM) or a bus from Observatorio bus station in Mexico City to Morelia. Taking a bus to Morelia from Observatorio bus station in Mexico City

Take a cab from “Benito Juarez” international airport (MEX) in Mexico City to the Observatorio bus station. Observatorio bus station is 45 minutes to 1 hour away from Mexico City's airport. Once you have arrived to Observatorio bus station, locate the bus services ETN or Primera Plus and purchase your tickets directly in the booth. Buses depart every 30 to 45 minutes to Morelia.
